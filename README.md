### 👋 Hi, I'm Danilo Fróes!
### Welcome to my GitHub profile!

- Engineering student at the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro (UFRJ).
- Passionate about technology, especially programming, robotics, and artificial intelligence.
- Currently the project leader of the Mini Sumo robot category at Minerva Bots, UFRJ's robotics competition team. Previously contributed to the Trekking and Mega Sumo (3kg) categories as part of the electronics and programming team.
- Scientific researcher at the Laboratory of Simulation and Optimization of Productive Systems (LASOS) at the National Institute of Technology (INT), where I develop a machine learning library for automating the testing and comparison of models.
- Participant in the Jovens Cientistas Cariocas program by the Rio de Janeiro City Hall, conducting a research project that applies machine learning to public health.
- Technician in Digital Game Programming through the technical high school program at Firjan SESI/SENAI.
